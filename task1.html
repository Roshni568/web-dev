About Us

Bevzilla was crafted with the thought of bringing beverages that are instant and on the go. It was launched in the market in 2020 with a vision to have a healthier and no preservative alternative. Hence the brand brought the most sought-after and delicious beverage experience in the form of cubes & powder. With a splendid palate of taste and a blend of different flavours, Bevzilla offers Instant Coffee Cubes & Milkshake Cubes sweetened with Date Palm Jaggery, Instant Coffee Powder that is made with 100% Pure Arabica Beans, and Instant Iced Tea Powder.
